Signals

Continuous capture lets AI see trends, not single points.

Heart Rate / PPG

Detects inflammation, stress, and cardiac load trends.

Respiration / Accelerometer

Captures faster breathing and pain-related posture changes.

Temperature

Combined with multivariate patterns to predict fever risk.

Activity / IMU

Sleep / Behavior
